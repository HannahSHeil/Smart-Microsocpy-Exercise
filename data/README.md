# 🧪 Dataset: HeLa Cells with Bacterial Infection (Widefield Microscopy)

## 📖 Description

This dataset contains multi-channel microscopy images of **HeLa cells infected with *Yersinia pseudotuberculosis***.

Images were acquired as large field-of-view overview scans consisting of **3×3 stitched fields**.  
One dataset is additionally **downsampled using 2×2 binning (median)**.

The dataset is designed for population-level analysis and smart microscopy workflows.

---

## 🔬 Imaging Channels

- **C1 – DIC**  
  Transmitted light imaging of cell morphology  

- **C2 – AF647 (Spy650–fastAct)**  
  Fluorescent labeling of the **actin cytoskeleton**  

- **C3 – GFP**  
  Fluorescent signal from **bacteria**

---

## ⚙️ Microscope Setup

- **Microscope:** Nikon Ti2-E inverted wide-field fluorescence microscope  
- **Environment:** BSL-2  
- **Objective:** Nikon Plan Apo λ 10×, NA 0.45  
- **Focus stabilization:** Perfect Focus System (PFS)  

---

## 📷 Camera

- Nikon DS-Qi2 CMOS camera
